Required Area and Structural Design</h3><p><strong>Population Basis:</strong> Approximately 30,000 people</p><ul>
<li>
<p>Living space: 25㎡ per person → 750,000㎡ total</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Food production space: 50㎡ per person → 1,500,000㎡ total</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Public facilities (schools, hospitals, shops): 10㎡ per person → 300,000㎡ total</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Infrastructure and mechanical systems: 15㎡ per person → 450,000㎡ total</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Total Required Area:</strong> Approximately 3,000,000㎡ (3㎢)</p>
</li>
</ul><p><strong>Structure Type:</strong> Circular UFO with a diameter of 2 to 4 km.<br>
A diameter of at least 2.5 to 3 km is sufficient for self-sufficiency for 30,000 people.</p><hr><h3>2. Essential Functional Zones</h3><p><strong>A. Residential Zone</strong></p><ul>
<li>
<p>High-density multi-story structures</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Mixed layout: private, family, and shared units</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Noise, vibration, and temperature control systems included</p>
</li>
</ul><p><strong>B. Food Production Zone</strong></p><ul>
<li>
<p>Multi-layer vertical farms with LED lighting</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Insect protein and cultured meat systems</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Automated waste-to-fertilizer recycling loop</p>
</li>
</ul><p><strong>C. Water and Waste Recycling</strong></p><ul>
<li>
<p>100% wastewater purification and reuse</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Air purification using plant-based systems and nano-filters</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>CO₂ capture and conversion via plants</p>
</li>
</ul><p><strong>D. Energy Independence</strong></p><ul>
<li>
<p>Fusion reactor or high-efficiency solar panels</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Power storage systems (e.g., superconducting batteries)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Backup bio-gas and hydrogen fuel cells</p>
</li>
</ul><p><strong>E. Medical and Regenerative Technology</strong></p><ul>
<li>
<p>Hospitals, clinics, pharmaceutical production</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>3D printing of artificial organs and tissues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Space medicine and radiation countermeasures</p>
</li>
</ul><p><strong>F. Education, Culture, and Mental Health</strong></p><ul>
<li>
<p>Schools, libraries, spaces for arts and therapy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Areas for religion, meditation, and personal reflection</p>
</li>
</ul><p><strong>G. Propulsion and Navigation Systems</strong></p><ul>
<li>
<p>Anti-gravity, ion, or nuclear propulsion engines</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Automatic and manual navigation systems</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Drones, exploration pods, and shuttlecraft</p>
